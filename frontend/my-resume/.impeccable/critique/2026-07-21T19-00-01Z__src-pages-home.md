## Anti-Patterns Verdict

**FAIL — still bears AI-generated tells.** Identical card grids, terminal motif, redundant subtitles, placeholder content, and uniform card hover lifts all remain.

## Overall Impression

The recent redesign moved the needle significantly — the gold-on-white palette, Spectral headings, and cleaner tokens give the site a more intentional feel. But the underlying template structure (identical project cards, placeholder data, terminal cliché, redundant copy) still reads as AI-generated scaffolding that hasn't been fully replaced.

## What's Working

1. Infra Animation — genuinely creative and memorable.
2. Color + Typography Direction — gold on pure white with Spectral is a strong, confident choice.
3. Experience Section Expand/Collapse — progressive disclosure done right.

## Priority Issues

### [P1] Fake/Placeholder Content Throughout
Projects section shows 6 generic projects with 404ing placeholder images. Contact social links href="#". Resume PDF 404s.

### [P1] Contact Form Submits to Nowhere
handleSubmit calls console.log() — data silently lost.

### [P2] Identical Card Grid in Projects
6 cards all identical structure. By the 3rd card users stop reading.

### [P2] Terminal Motif Clashes with Brand
$ terraform apply + macOS traffic-light dots in hard-coded colors is the most generic developer portfolio trope.

### [P2] Redundant Section Subtitles
Every section has a subtitle that restates the heading.

### [P3] Terminal Noise Animation is Choppy
animation: noiseShift 0.5s steps(3) infinite — only 3 frames.

### [P3] InfraAnimation Inline Styles Have Undocumented Colors
6 undocumented colors and font sizes outside DESIGN.md ramp.
